Thank you for your interest in Kaizen IBBA. We offer one of the finest martial arts programs available in Northern Colorado. From our Lil’ Dragons and Tiny Tigers classes to our Youth and Adult classes, we have a program to meet most people’s needs and budgets. We offer daytime, afternoon, and evening classes Monday through Saturday. Our classes are divided by age as well as ability level and we pride ourselves on being “The Family School” where every student feels welcome and appreciated.
